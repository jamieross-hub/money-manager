info Family Group Modes

account_balance_wallet

Common Mode

All family expenses and balances are managed collectively. This mode is ideal for families who share most of their expenses equally without needing to track exact who-owes-who breakdowns for every transaction.

call_split

Split Mode

Expenses can be split precisely either by percentage, shares, or exact amounts among members. Perfect for groups or roommates who need to maintain accurate individual tabs and settle up specific balances.

The group mode can be changed at any time by the family admin in the settings.
