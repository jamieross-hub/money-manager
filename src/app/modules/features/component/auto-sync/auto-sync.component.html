Setup Auto Sync

Connect your bank accounts by forwarding transaction emails to our secure tracker. Follow the steps below.

info Gmail Forwarding Steps

1

Open Gmail Settings

On your computer, click the settings icon and select "See all settings".

2

Go to Forwarding Tab

Click the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab at the top.

3

Add Forwarding Address

Click "Add a forwarding address" and enter:

mail {{forwardingEmail}}
4

Verify and Enable

Confirm the code sent to us, then select "Forward a copy of incoming mail..." and click "Save Changes" at the bottom.

lightbulb Pro Tip: Use Filters

To protect your privacy, create a filter in Gmail to only forward bank-related emails (e.g., from:alerts@yourbank.com).
