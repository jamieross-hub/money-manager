Requesting Data Deletion

At Money Manager, we value your privacy and provide you with the option to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove your personal information, financial records, budgets, and all other data linked to your account from our active databases.

How to Delete Your Data

You can request the deletion of your data through the following methods:

1. Via Email (Recommended)

Send an email from your registered email address to {{ contactEmail }} with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include your full name and the email address associated with your account.

2. In-App Request

Go to Settings > Profile > Delete Account within the application. You will be asked to confirm your choice before the process begins.

What Happens After Your Request?

  • Processing Time: Most deletion requests are processed within 48 to 72 hours.
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been permanently deleted.
  • Data Retention: Some minimal information may be retained if required for legal, tax, or fraud prevention purposes, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
  • Irreversibility: Please note that once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Any subscriptions or premium features will be terminated.
